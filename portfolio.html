The Perils of the Arch was created to show the current and historical range of the Spotted Turtle in the Frontenac Arch to raise awareness about Spotted Turtle range declines. The map was designed for print at a scale of 44in x 34in with the general public as the target audience. This map was created independently as a final project for Geovisualization I at Fleming College where the goal of the project was to utilize cartographic skills to depict a topic of your choice using primarily vector data.

The purpose of this map is to showcase the land use types withing St. John's, Newfoundland with an emphasis on natural areas. The map was designed as an informative map for potential tourists about the unique landscape that surrounds St. John's, Newfoundland, and some of the adventurous activities they could partake in. The map was designed as a printed map with a printed size of 44in x 34in. The City of Adventure was a greyscale map created in Geovisualization I at Fleming College as part of an assignment.
